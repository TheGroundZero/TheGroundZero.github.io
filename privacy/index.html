Privacy Statement

Last update: 2021-12-18

Who we are ¶

Our website address is: https://sequr.be.

For any queries related to this privacy policy or to exercise your rights, e-mail privacy@sequr.be.

What personal data we collect and why we collect it ¶

IP address ¶

The use of an IP address is inherent to how the internet works.

When you visit the website, your IP address may end up in the logs of the webserver(s) hosting this website and – if applicable – that of Content Delivery Networks (CDN) used to cache the website.

On its own, an IP address is not sufficient to uniquely identify a person. It can however reveal information about your general location (e.g. country, state or city).

If you wish to hide the origin of your network traffic, you can use a VPN. Be aware however that a VPN provider may log your personal IP as well as the websites you visit.

Embedded content from other websites ¶

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. videos, images, articles, etc.). Embedded content from other websites behaves in the exact same way as if the visitor has visited the other website.

These websites may collect data about you, use cookies, embed additional third-party tracking, and monitor your interaction with that embedded content, including tracking your interaction with the embedded content if you have an account and are logged in to that website.

We will always try our best to enable all privacy features third-parties allow us to configure when including their content. However, it’s up to the content provider to decide whether they provide such configuration to website owners.

We’ve configured Hugo to provide as much privacy as possible. For more info, see Hugo and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Cookies ¶

We do not make use of cookies.

Browser fingerprinting ¶

We do not partake in any form of browser fingerprinting.

We explicitly opt-out of Google’s Federated Learning of Cohorts (FLoC) (more info).

Affiliate links ¶

Articles may reference products on external webshops using affiliate links. When using these links, the external webshop will link your purchase to your visit to this website. These links do not add any additional cost to your purchase, but provide a kick-back to the site owners.

To get the non-affiliate link, open the affiliate link in a private browser window and remove all parameters.

What rights you have over your data ¶

If you can provide a method of verifying your identity, you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, if applicable. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Where we send your data ¶

We will never commercialize, sell or share your personal data with third parties.

No warrants have ever been served to sequr.be, or sequr.be principals or employees.
No searches or seizures of any kind have ever been performed on sequr.be assets.
