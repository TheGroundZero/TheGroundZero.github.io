Disabling Quick Glance (Discover) on OnePlus

For some reason, OnePlus OS12 added the Discover a.k.a. Quick Glance view to the homescreen. The idea is you swipe right on the home screen to get an overview of stuff that might be of interest to you.

Many people, including myself, don’t use this view and get frustrated when they accidentally swipe right from the home screen. What’s even worse, is that there’s no option in the OnePlus settings to disable Quick Glance.

Luckily, there’s a solution!

Here’s how to disable Quick Glance on a OnePlus OS12 device:

  1. Download and install the Quick Glance APK from OPPO
  2. Press and hold on the Home Screen
  3. Press More
  4. Scroll down to Quick Glance
  5. Turn off the toggle button

Quick Glance will now be gone from your home screen and you’ll never have to worry about accidentally swiping right every again!
